## 1. Hot Shots Program

![Hot Shots – Kids on Court](https://lirp.cdn-website.com/35785d70/dms3rep/multi/opt/A43R9486-1920w.jpg)

ANZ Tennis Hot Shots is tennis for kids! It's a program designed to help every child, no matter their age or ability, jump in and start playing tennis. ANZ Tennis Hot Shots is played on smaller courts with modified equipment, including lighter racquets, lower nets and low compression balls that don't bounce too high.

With four stages available at Tennis Blast, qualified Tennis Australia coaches will help guide your kids every step of the way.

Squads / Age Groups:

- Launch PAD – Blue Ball (ages 3–5)
- Red Ball (ages 5–8)
- Orange Ball (ages 8–10)
- Green Ball (ages 9+)
- Club Tennis

## HEAD Racquets

### HEAD Speed

This racquet is perfect for all-court players looking for a good balance between power and control along with an arm-friendly feel and easy access to spin.

### HEAD Extreme

The Extreme is ideal for the big-swinging, modern player looking for loads of power and spin.

### HEAD Radical

Launched in the 90s with the help of Andre Agassi, the Radical family continues to evolve for the modern game. Defined by its solid feel and controllable power, the Radical line has something for every ability level.

### HEAD Prestige

Given their heavier weights and smaller head sizes, Prestige racquets are best suited to intermediate and advanced players, especially those with solid mechanics who demand a high level of precision.

## Babolat Racquets

### Babolat Pure Drive

Fast feeling and easy to handle, the latest Pure Drives bring spin-friendly performance to every shot. A selection of weights, lengths and head sizes means there's a Pure Drive to fit all levels from beginner through advanced.

### Babolat Boost Drive

Babolat's Boost series features great options for beginner and intermediate players. This series is not only recommended to bargain hunters in search of a performance racquet, but it's also great for players new to the game and looking for great all-around playability.

## Wilson Racquets

### Wilson Blade

Blade racquets come in a variety of weights, head sizes and string patterns, which means they have something for every ability level. They tend to be more arm-friendly and less powerful than Ultra and Burn racquets.

### Wilson Clash

The revolution is here! The Clash series is Wilson's most technologically advanced racquet line. These racquets not only offer some of the lowest and most arm-friendly flex points in the industry, but they achieve this without compromising power or stability.
